Key Features

Encrypted Local Storage

All your financial data is encrypted and stored securely on your device. Your privacy is protected with industry-standard encryption.

Optional Cloud Sync

Choose to sync your data securely to Firebase when needed, or keep everything local for maximum privacy.

Multi-Method Tracking

Track expenses across multiple payment methods including cash, cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers.

Weekly Analytics

Comprehensive trend analysis with beautiful, animated charts showing your spending patterns and category breakdowns.

Accessible Interface

Built with accessibility best practices, ensuring the app is usable by everyone regardless of abilities.

Category Management

Organize expenses into custom categories with detailed breakdowns and spending insights.
