Transaction Data Deletion

Reset Your Financial Data

Learn how to permanently delete all your transaction data, including payments, deposits, and loans from Trackify. This action cannot be undone.

Important Warning

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your transaction data will be permanently removed from the app and our servers.

1

Steps to Delete Transaction Data

1

Open the Trackify App

Launch the Trackify app on your mobile device.

2

Navigate to Settings

Go to the Settings screen, accessible from the main menu or profile section.

3

Find Finance Overview

Scroll to the "Finance Overview" section in the settings.

4

Tap Clear All Transactions

Select the "Clear All Transactions" option.

5

Read Confirmation Dialog

Carefully read the confirmation dialog explaining that this will delete all transactions and loans.

6

Confirm Deletion

Tap "Delete" to confirm and permanently remove all transaction data.

2
