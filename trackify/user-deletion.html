User Account Deletion

Permanently Delete Your Account

Learn how to request complete deletion of your Trackify account and all associated data. This includes a 7-day grace period for reconsideration.

Important Warning

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone after the grace period. You have 7 days to cancel the deletion request.

1

Steps to Request Account Deletion

1

Open the Trackify App

Launch the Trackify app on your mobile device.

2

Navigate to Settings

Go to the Settings screen, accessible from the main menu or profile section.

3

Find Support & About

Scroll to the "Support & About" section in the settings.

4

Tap Delete Account

Select the "Delete Account" option.

5

Read Confirmation Dialog

Carefully read the confirmation dialog explaining the 7-day grace period.

6

Confirm Deletion Request

Tap "Request Deletion" to initiate the account deletion process.

2

What Happens After Requesting Deletion

Immediate Logout

Your account will be marked for deletion, and you will be logged out immediately.

Local Data Cleared

All local data on your device (e.g., cached transactions) will be cleared right away.

7-Day Grace Period

You have 7 days to cancel the deletion by logging back into the app. If you log in within 7 days, the deletion request will be canceled, and your data will be restored.

Permanent Deletion

After 7 days, if not canceled, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

3

Types of Data Deleted

User Profile

User profile information including name, email, and account details.

Transaction Records

All transaction records including payments, deposits, and loans.

User-Generated Content

Any other user-generated content stored in the app.

4

Data Retained

No Personal Data
