Trackify Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

Trackify is a personal finance application (mobile) that helps users record and visualize incomes, expenses, balances and trends. This Privacy Policy explains what information Trackify collects, how it is used, how it is stored and your choices regarding that information. This policy applies to the Trackify application and any related services provided by the developer.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account & Identity Data: When you sign in using Firebase Authentication we store your user identifier (UID) and the display name / email provided by the authentication provider. These are used to identify your account and to derive an encryption key for local data.
  • Transaction Data: Transaction records you create (amount, category, date/time, notes, method, etc.) are stored locally and may be synced to Cloud Firestore if you enable sync. This includes only the transaction fields required for functionality.
  • Device & Usage Data: Basic app usage and crash data (if enabled) to help debug and improve the app. Trackify does not collect tracking identifiers for advertising purposes.

3. How We Store Your Data

  • Local Storage: Trackify uses an encrypted local store (Hive) to keep transaction and user data on your device for offline access and fast reads.
  • Deterministic Encryption: Local data is encrypted with AES-256 using a deterministic key derived from the authenticated user's UID (SHA-256). This design allows the app to recover and decrypt local data after an app data clear or reinstall provided you re-authenticate with the same account. The raw UID is used only to derive the key and is not stored in plain text insecurely.
  • Remote Storage (optional): If you enable cloud sync, your encrypted data will be stored in your Firebase project's Cloud Firestore.

4. Why We Use This Data

  • Provide core functionality: to record, display and analyze your finances.
  • Sync: to keep data in sync across devices when using the cloud sync feature.
  • Improve the app: aggregated, non-personally-identifying usage data and crash reports help improve stability and features.

5. Sharing & Third Parties

Trackify does not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

We may use third-party services to operate the app (e.g., Firebase for authentication and database, crash-reporting tools). These providers process data solely to provide their services and are governed by their own privacy policies.

If you explicitly export data (CSV, backup) or share screenshots, you control that export and any recipients.

6. Security

We use strong encryption (AES-256 via the `encrypt` package) for local data. The deterministic key approach balances user data recoverability with per-user isolation — but like any design, it has trade-offs; keep your account credentials secure.

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. Be careful with device-level security (PIN/biometrics) and backups.

7. Your Choices

  • Sign-in & Deletion: You can delete your account (and associated remote data) via the app's settings if supported; deleting your account does not automatically remove local backups unless the app provides that flow. Check settings for account deletion and local data wipe options.
  • Cloud Sync: You can opt-in or opt-out of cloud sync in the app settings. If you opt out, your data remains local only.
  • Export: You can export your transaction data manually; exports are not automatically shared.

8. Children

Trackify is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. If material changes are made, we will update the "Last updated" date in this document.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request data deletion, contact: mahesamun@gmail.com
