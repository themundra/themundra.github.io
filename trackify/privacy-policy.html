Overview

Trackify is a personal finance application (mobile) that helps users record and visualize incomes, expenses, balances and trends. This Privacy Policy explains what information Trackify collects, how it is used, how it is stored and your choices regarding that information. This policy applies to the Trackify application and any related services provided by the developer.

2

Data We Collect

Account & Identity Data

When you sign in using Firebase Authentication we store your user identifier (UID) and the display name / email provided by the authentication provider. These are used to identify your account and to derive an encryption key for local data.

Transaction Data

Transaction records you create (amount, category, date/time, notes, method, etc.) are stored locally and may be synced to Cloud Firestore if you enable sync. This includes only the transaction fields required for functionality.

Device & Usage Data

Basic app usage and crash data (if enabled) to help debug and improve the app. Trackify does not collect tracking identifiers for advertising purposes.

3

How We Store Your Data

üì±

Local Storage

Trackify uses an encrypted local store (Hive) to keep transaction and user data on your device for offline access and fast reads.

üîê

Deterministic Encryption

Local data is encrypted with AES-256 using a deterministic key derived from the authenticated user's UID (SHA-256). This design allows the app to recover and decrypt local data after an app data clear or reinstall provided you re-authenticate with the same account.

Remote Storage (optional)

If you enable cloud sync with an authenticated account, your encrypted data will be stored in your Firebase project's Cloud Firestore as long as you're connected to the internet. Remote storage is not available for guest login users.

4

Why We Use This Data

  • Provide core functionality: to record, display and analyze your finances.
  • Sync: to keep data in sync across devices when using the cloud sync feature.
  • Improve the app: aggregated, non-personally-identifying usage data and crash reports help improve stability and features.
5

Sharing & Third Parties

Our Commitment

Trackify does not sell or rent personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to operate the app (e.g., Firebase for authentication and database, crash-reporting tools). These providers process data solely to provide their services and are governed by their own privacy policies.

Your Control

You can share screenshots of your data. CSV and backup export features are not available as of now. When export features become available, you will control that export and any recipients.

6

Security

Strong Encryption

We use strong encryption (AES-256 via the `encrypt` package) for both local and remote data. The deterministic key approach balances user data recoverability with per-user isolation.

üîí

Protection Measures

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. Be careful with device-level security (PIN/biometrics) and backups.

7

Your Choices

Sign-in & Deletion

You can delete your account (and associated remote data) via the app's settings if supported; deleting your account does not automatically remove local backups unless the app provides that flow.

Cloud Sync

For authenticated users, cloud sync happens automatically when connected to the internet. Guest login users do not have access to cloud sync and their data remains local only.

8

Children

Trackify is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
